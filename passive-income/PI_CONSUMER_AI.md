# Platform 4 — Consumer AI Products & Entertainment

## What It Is
Vertical AI agents and entertainment tools built for specific passionate audiences. All subscription-based or one-time purchase. All autonomous after launch. CB is not in the loop once the product is live.

## The Validation Rule
Before any build starts — confirm demand with a warm signal from CB's actual network. The cheapest validation is a direct question to 10 people: "Would you pay $X/month for this?" Five yeses = green light. Zero yeses = scrap or pivot before spending a day on code.

---

## Product 1 — Sports Intelligence Platform

**What**: AI-powered platform delivering game intelligence, betting angles, technique coaching tips (golf swing mechanics, baseball hitting, basketball shooting), and live coverage insights.

**Audience**: Sports fans who want an edge — amateur athletes, fantasy players, casual bettors, coaches.

**CB's Edge**: Real sports connections in the network = warm launch audience. CB's AI mastery = analysis quality others can't match manually.

**Model**: Freemium → $9–$15/month subscription

**Sub-features (each a potential upgrade tier)**:
- Game intelligence and outcome analysis
- Technique tips — swing mechanics, shooting form, batting stance
- Betting / horse racing data and value angle identification
- Live coverage insights and real-time analysis

**Validate first**:
- [ ] Poll 20 people in sports network: "Would you pay $10/month for AI-powered sports intelligence — tips, picks, and game analysis?"
- [ ] If 5 say yes → build MVP
- [ ] If less → adjust positioning, re-ask

**Audience**: Homeowners, garden enthusiasts, anyone with a yard or indoor plants.

**Model**: $4–$9/month subscription

**MVP Scope (4 features)**:

2. Personalized care schedule generator
3. Local weather API integration for watering alerts
4. Monthly seasonal task checklist by growing zone

**Actions**:

- [ ] Identify weather API (OpenWeatherMap or similar)

## Product 8 — Quad Box (Multi-Streaming Aggregator)

**What**: View content from multiple streaming services simultaneously in a customizable grid — sports, news, entertainment — without switching between apps.

**Audience**: Sports fans watching multiple games, traders watching multiple news feeds, power streamers managing 3+ subscriptions.

**Model**: One-time purchase $9.99 or subscription $4.99/month

**Research gate**: Streaming platform APIs have complex and restrictive licensing terms. Verify what's technically and legally buildable before committing to this product. Do not start build until research is complete.

**Actions**:
- [ ] Research streaming API licensing for Netflix, ESPN, YouTube, Apple TV+
- [ ] Determine if web-based or native app is the viable path
- [ ] Only proceed if legal and technically feasible

## Product 9 — TV Sound / Dim Fix

**What**: App that normalizes television audio — boosts dialogue, reduces overblown music and sound effects, brings balance back to the listening experience.

**Audience**: Everyone who has ever turned on subtitles because they couldn't hear the dialogue.

**Model**: Freemium → $2.99/month or one-time $9.99
