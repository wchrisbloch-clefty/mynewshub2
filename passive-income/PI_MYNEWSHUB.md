# Platform 1 — MyNewsHub

## What It Is
An AI-powered news and information hub that curates, summarizes, and organizes content across categories — eliminating noise, surfacing signal, filtering fact from spin. The active repo is MyNewsHub2 (React/Vite). The product is being built. The monetization layer needs to be defined now, before the next feature is written.

## CB's Edge
- Product is already in development — no cold start, no idea stage
- CB personally lives this pain point daily
- Full network = warm launch audience, zero ad spend required
- AI curation + security/privacy knowledge = differentiated product others can't easily replicate
- MyNewsHub is the natural front door for every other platform

---

## The Core Principle
> Stop building features before defining the paid tier. Every new feature should either drive free-to-paid conversion or increase paid retention. If it does neither, it goes to the backlog.

---

## Monetization Architecture

### Free Tier — The Hook
- Up to 5 custom news feeds
- Standard AI summaries
- Daily digest email
- Watermarked access to premium features — creates friction and visibility

### Paid Tier — $12/month or $99/year
- Unlimited custom feeds
- Full AI summary engine — no limits
- Media vs. facts source-rating layer
- AI updates vertical feed
- Tax advantaged / financial changes feed
- Social hub layer
- Life organization layer
- Priority processing and faster refresh
- No watermarks

### Founding Member Offer — $79/year (Launch This Week)
- Locked at founding rate for life
- Full paid tier access
- Direct input on product roadmap
- Named as a founding member
- **Launch before the product is complete** — this is the first revenue event
- Collect via Stripe, Gumroad, or direct payment — no engineering required

### Affiliate Layer — Passive Commission, Zero Marginal Work
Set up once. Earns automatically on every click and conversion.

| Category | Target Partners | Commission Model |
|---|---|---|
| AI Tools | Claude Pro, Perplexity, Notion, Zapier | Per signup / recurring % |
| Financial Products | Brokerage platforms, tax tools, financial apps | Per account open |
| Golf | Equipment brands, course booking tools | Per sale |
| Sports | Data providers, streaming services | Per signup |
| Privacy & Security | VPN services, identity protection tools | Per subscription |
| News & Media | Substack, Beehiiv | Per referral |

### White-Label Licensing — B2B, Post-MVP
License the curation and AI summary engine to:
- Local news organizations
- Independent newsletters scaling their tech
- Media companies wanting the AI layer without building it
- Target: $500–$2,000/month per license
- 10 licenses = $5,000–$20,000/month, fully automated
