## Primary Strategy — BRRRR

**Buy → Rehab → Rent → Refinance → Repeat**

| Phase | Goal | CB's Actions |
|---|---|---|
| **Buy** | Acquire below 75% of ARV before rehab costs — distressed, off-market, or listed with equity built in | Network for off-market flow; MLS for listed deals; wholesaler relationships |
| **Rehab** | Bring to rental-ready standard — maximize ARV without over-improving for the rental market | Reliable contractor relationship defined before first deal |
| **Rent** | Place a qualified tenant immediately — vacancy is the enemy | Property manager handles tenant screening, placement, and lease from day one |
| **Refinance** | Cash-out refi at new appraised value — pull most or all of original capital back out | Portfolio lender relationship established before first deal |
| **Repeat** | Recycled capital goes directly into next acquisition — scale without additional out-of-pocket capital | Each deal funds the next if executed correctly |

## Deal Criteria — Non-Negotiable

| Criteria | Target | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Purchase price | Below 75% of ARV before rehab | Protects equity from day one |
| Gross rent multiplier | Rent ≥ 1% of total acquisition cost | Ensures cash flow math works |
| Cap rate | 7%+ on stabilized value | Minimum return threshold |
| Cash-on-cash (post-refi) | 8%+ | After all expenses and mortgage |
| Rehab budget | Defined and locked before offer is submitted | No open-ended scopes — ever |
| Hold period | 5+ years minimum | Let appreciation and paydown compound |
| Vacancy assumption | 8% (1 month/year) | Conservative underwriting only |
| CapEx reserve | 5% of annual rent | Roof, HVAC, major systems |

## Autonomy Architecture

**Goal**: Rental income that hits CB's account without CB's time each month.

| Function | Who Handles It |
|---|---|
| Tenant screening and placement | Property management company |
| Rent collection | PM company — automatic ACH to CB |
| Maintenance coordination | PM company |
| Late notices and evictions | PM company |
| Monthly reporting | PM company provides statement |
| Annual review and decision | CB — once per year per property |

**CB's recurring time commitment after close**: Review monthly statement (5 minutes). Annual review with PM (30 minutes). That is the job.

**PM fee**: Typically 8–12% of monthly rent. Built into underwriting — never surprises.

## Tax Structure Consideration

- Rental income is eligible for depreciation deduction — consult CPA to set up correctly from deal one
- LLC structure (or series LLC in Texas) for liability protection and clean accounting
- BRRRR cash-out refis are not taxable events — capital recycled tax-free until sale
- 1031 exchange on exit — defer capital gains when selling and rolling into next property
- Cost segregation study on rehab properties — accelerates depreciation deductions
